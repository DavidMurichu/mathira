Contacting the MP's Office

To book an appointment with the MP in Mathira, residents can contact the office through phone 0706 202 203 or email us info@Mathiraconstitueny.com The office staff will then provide available dates for the meeting and schedule an appointment.

Confirming the Appointment

After booking the appointment, residents are required to confirm the meeting by providing personal details such as their name and ID number. This helps in verifying their identity and ensuring that the meeting is productive.
