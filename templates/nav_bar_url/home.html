Welcome to Mathira Constituency

Mission

To transform the lives of the people of Mathira Constituency by professionally managing available resources as well as create an enabling environment for the economic empowerment and prosperity of all constituents.

Vision

To be a truly developed constituency where every individual has an equal opportunity to prosper.

Mathira Constituency Overview

Mathira is a constituency located in Nyeri County, Kenya. It is known for its diverse population and rich cultural heritage. The constituency is committed to improving the lives of its residents through various initiatives in education, healthcare, infrastructure, and community development.

The representative of Mathira constituency is dedicated to serving the community and addressing the needs and concerns of its residents.

OUR TOP AGENDA GOALS

Agriculture

  • Enhanced Agricultural Productivity and Sustainability
  • Rural Farmer Empowerment and Capacity Building
  • Diversified Crop Production and Market Access
  • Investment in Agri-Infrastructure and Technology
  • Climate Resilience and Disaster Preparedness

Health

  • Improved Healthcare Access and Affordability
  • Enhanced Healthcare Infrastructure and Facilities
  • Health Education and Awareness Programs
  • Disease Prevention and Vaccination Campaigns
  • Mental Health Awareness and Support Services

Inflastracture

  • Modernization and Maintenance of Roads and Highways
  • Improved Public Transportation Systems
  • Upgrading and Expanding Energy and Power Infrastructure
  • Enhanced Water and Sanitation Facilities
  • Development of Smart Cities and Urban Planning

Youth Empowerment

  • Educational Advancement and Skill Development
  • Employment Opportunities and Job Creation
  • Entrepreneurship and Small Business Support
  • Access to Mentorship and Guidance Programs
  • Engagement in Civic Participation and Leadership

Men Empowerment

  • Promote Positive Masculinity and Gender Equality
  • Support Men's Mental and Emotional Health
  • Advocate for Men's Health and Well-being
  • Offer Skill Development and Employment Opportunities
  • Address Social and Cultural Stereotypes Affecting Men

Women Empowerment

  • Empower Women Economically and Promote Entrepreneurship

SMEs

  • Facilitate Access to Funding and Financial Support
  • Provide Training and Capacity Building for SMEs

Urban Renovtion

  • Revitalize Urban Infrastructure and Public Spaces
  • Promote Sustainable Urban Planning and Design
  • Upgrade Housing and Improve Living Conditions
